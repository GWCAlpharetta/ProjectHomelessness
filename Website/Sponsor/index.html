Sponsors

Our Mission: By using our time, money, and resources, we will help the unfortunate to find jobs. To become a donor, you must have your first and last name, middle name optional (ex. John Doe), a pre-meditated amount of money to donate (ex. $100), a pre-meditated reason (or for no reason at all) to donate, and the love in your heart to donate to the unfortunate.If you don't have time to volunteer or jobs to hand out to those who need it, but still want to help, you're in luck! You can contribute to those who need it by donating! The money will be sent to help people get access to this website, fund homelessness shelters, and will be sent to fundraisers to eradicate homelessness.

What Can You do to Help?


It's okay if you don't want to volunteer or don't have any jobs for those who need it. You can still help!
